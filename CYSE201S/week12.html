Prompt

Read the sample breach letter “SAMPLE DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION” and describe how two different economics theories and two different social sciences theories relate to the letter.

Economics Theories

Theory of Rational Expectations

Customers have to make decisions based on their expectations.
They would expect their personal information would be protected from unauthorized access.
The decisions they make influence the economic outcome for the breached company.

Monetary Theory

This breach has a negative impact on the economy.
Losses incurred by the platform provider, customers, and company result in a reduction in the money supply.
All parties involved also have to decide how and where to spend more money to aid in recovery.

Social Sciences Theories

Conflict Theory

Criminals broke into the company's servers and utilized malware to steal credit card information.
This attack is a conflict between intruders and customers to obtain payment data for financial resources.
The situation represents a competition between groups over limited resources.

Social Capital Theory

Social Capital Theory emphasizes the importance of trust.
Relationships between company and customer are delicate - negative circumstances can destroy the social capital.
The purpose of the notice of breach is to provide transparency in the details and establish trust.
