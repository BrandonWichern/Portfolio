Prompt

Visit PrivacyRights.org to see the types of publicly available information about data breaches.
How might researchers use this information to study breaches?

Researchers can study trends in the most common types of breaches. They can see which industries, and specifically which organizations, report the highest rates in different areas. Many breaches contain detailed descriptions of the incidents with cited sources. This information could be used to find similarities among the same or different types of breaches. I would caution that a list like this being publicly available could provide a list of potential targets for cyber criminals in the future. Not all researchers have good intentions.
