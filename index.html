Data Analysis?

Career Foundry was advertising a bootcamp for data analysis.
They offered a free 5 day course, so I decided to check it out.

I had a blast and wanted to know more. YouTube certainly had plenty to say about the subject. A common thread I came across was Coursera. The Google Data Analytics course was recommended as a good starting point for learning more about the thought processes and tools involved.

I realized I had always thought this way and my previous job experience was very transferrable to this field : gathering data, filtering it, and drawing conclusions. Translating this passion into a career would be a dream come true.

This website was created to document my progression through my computer science studies. The foundational skills are relative to a variety of fields, so let's see where they lead.
