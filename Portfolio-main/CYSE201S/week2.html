Prompt

Explain how the principles of science relate to cybersecurity.

Science involves testing knowledge through experiment. Experimentation can generate knowledge as well. In any context, the desired outcome is the advancement of what is known. The principles of science are relatable to cybersecurity in a variety of ways. Keeping explanations or solutions simple, ethical, and based on evidence without bias is vital in any process. The modern age of mass connectivity allows us to share and repair the vulnerabilities that arise from strides towards improvement. Studying past events is required to ensure the efforts applied fit correctly within a given context. A questioning attitude ties it all together and promotes validating findings.
